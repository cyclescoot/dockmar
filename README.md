# Dockmar — Your AI Personal Dashboard

An open-source AI-powered personal dashboard. 
Open it every morning. Everything updates automatically.

## What it does
- Live weather for your city
- Today's calendar events
- AI-curated news tailored to your interests
- Daily briefing written fresh each morning
- Finance analyzer — drag in your bank CSV monthly
- The Council — your personal AI advisory panel
- Cut the Subscription — replace paid services with AI

## How to use it
Visit the live dashboard, choose your AI provider, 
enter your free API key, and configure each card.
Everything saves to your browser. Nothing stored 
on any server.

## AI Providers supported
- Anthropic Claude (most capable, live web search)
- OpenAI GPT-4o
- Google Gemini (free tier available — recommended for new users)
